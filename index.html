HISTORY

Misha Ivanov created the angst font in 2023, inspired by the conflict in Ukraine. Ivanov noticed a signage commemorating the friendship between Ukraine and Russia hanging in an Moscow metro station in February of 2022. This font includes two axis of variability and six basic variations. This font has similar characteristics to other serif fonts, such as Times New Roman, yet are much more versatile and striking then them. The variable axes allow for more flexibility in designing posters, websites and other things where typography is desired.
